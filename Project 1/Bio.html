Mateo Navarrette was borned and raised in Los Angeles California. Naturally, he turned into a fiercely loyal fan of the Los Angeles Lakers and Dodgers. He got his start in sportswriting in 2018 when he joined the staff of Steemsports, the biggest sportswriting account on the Steemit blockchain. He has since expanded his writing onto quality sportswriting sites such as Scorum, Last Word On Pro Basketball and Crownhoops. Mateo has covered baseball, football, and soccer throughout his career, but his true passion lies with the NBA.

Mateo gets the most joy out of writing articles that are big-picture, analytical, and have a long shelf-life, as opposed to fleeting news articles where the sole purpose is to collect views. Follow Mateo on Twitter for a steady stream of thought-provoking content, laughs, and memes. He's one of the last people on the internet who is capable of having a civilized discussion with you - even if you're a Celtics fan.
