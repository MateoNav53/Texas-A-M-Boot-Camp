Mateo Navarrette

Sports Writer


Bio

Mateo Navarrette was borned and raised in Los Angeles California. Naturally, he grew up to become a fiercely loyal fan of the Los Angeles Lakers and Dodgers. He got his start in sportswriting in 2018 when he joined the staff of Steemsports, a sports account on the Steemit blockchain dedicated to covering sports news across the globe. He has since expanded his writing onto noted sites such as Scorum, Last Word on Sports and Crownhoops. Mateo has covered baseball, football, and soccer throughout his career, but his true passion lies with the NBA. Follow Mateo on Twitter for a steady stream of thought-provoking content, laughs, and memes. He's one of the last people on the internet who is capable of having a civilized discussion with you - even if you're a Celtics fan.
